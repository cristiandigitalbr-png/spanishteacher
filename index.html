About your teacher

Hi, I'm Cristian.

I'm a native Spanish speaker from Córdoba, Argentina, and I've spent more than ten years helping people around the world speak Spanish with real confidence.

I've taught students preparing for job interviews, moving abroad, traveling through Latin America, and simply wanting to connect more deeply with Spanish-speaking friends and family. Every one of them started in a different place — and that's exactly why I don't teach a fixed curriculum.

My approach blends natural conversation, grammar explained in context, and real-life situations, so learning feels practical, dynamic, and — yes — genuinely enjoyable. You won't just study Spanish. You'll use it.
